Welcome!

After over a year on the Ice, I was very fortunate to be able to travel and visit several countries as I "traveled my way home." I have now been to all seven continents, but I am to be back home and working on a long term career somewhere not quite so cold!

I have many interests and hobbies, including travel, technology, Linux, python, Music, general aviation, and various other things I like to work on as time permits.
